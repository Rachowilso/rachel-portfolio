About Me

I’m Rachel Wilson, a versatile and dynamic producer with extensive experience across various industries including advertising, fitness, non-profit, and post-production. Over the years, I’ve spearheaded projects from concept to completion for major brands, non-profits, and independent productions. My work spans commercial production, internal company training videos, event and fundraising content, and social media campaigns.

With a background in business management and production coordination, I bring a unique mix of creativity, leadership, and logistical expertise to every project I manage. Whether it's directing multi-day film shoots, coordinating large-scale marketing videos for brands like Xponential Fitness, or producing heartfelt non-profit campaigns for organizations like Miracle for Kids and Unconditional Dog Rescue, I am passionate about storytelling through impactful media.

Additionally, I’ve co-founded businesses in veterinary services and landscape design, which further highlights my entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to quality in any field I work in. Currently, I work as a freelance producer, collaborating with agencies and brands to deliver top-tier production results.
